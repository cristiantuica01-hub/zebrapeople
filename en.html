Success Fee / Headhunting

Specialised recruitment for hard-to-fill roles, management positions and niche-skill professionals. We identify, approach and evaluate relevant candidates — including professionals who are not actively on the market and do not apply to job postings.

How it works
  • We analyse the role, company context and ideal candidate profile.
  • We conduct talent mapping and identify relevant candidates in the market.
  • We approach candidates directly and validate their experience, motivation and salary expectations.
  • We present only candidates who are interested and aligned with the role requirements.
  • We manage the entire process through to offer acceptance and candidate onboarding.
When it's the right fit
  • For management or leadership roles.
  • For specialists difficult to find through conventional recruitment methods.
  • For confidential positions.
  • For companies without sufficient internal time or resources.
  • For roles where speed and shortlist quality are critical.
Benefits
  • Access to candidates who are not actively job-seeking.
  • Structured and predictable recruitment process.
  • Reduced time investment from your internal team.
  • Preliminary candidate evaluation and interest validation.
  • Access to relevant market intelligence and salary benchmarks.
Engagement terms
  • Success fee: 10% of the hired candidate's gross annual salary.
  • Retainer: 15–25% of the estimated invoice value, paid at project launch.
  • Balance payment: due on the candidate's first day of employment.
  • Replacement guarantee in accordance with agreed contractual terms.
  • Final payment is made exclusively upon a successful hire.

Recruitment Subscription

A service designed for companies that hire continuously and need a partner that functions as an extension of their internal team. Instead of paying per hire, you get ongoing recruitment support through a fixed monthly retainer, with clear objectives, priorities and KPIs.

How it works
  • We take over part or all of your recruitment process.
  • We define target profiles and candidate attraction strategy.
  • We build and manage talent pipelines.
  • We contact, evaluate and coordinate candidates throughout the process.
  • We provide regular reports and full visibility on progress.
What's included
  • Profile definition and job posting creation.
  • Talent sourcing and active candidate outreach.
  • Screening and initial interviews.
  • Interview scheduling and coordination.
  • Salary benchmarking and market intelligence.
  • Offer formulation support and end-to-end process management.
When it's the right fit
  • The company has multiple open roles simultaneously.
  • Recruitment volume is consistent.
  • The internal team lacks sufficient capacity.
  • Cost predictability is a priority.
  • The company is looking for a long-term partner, not just one-off support.
Benefits
  • Predictable monthly cost.
  • Continuous access to recruitment expertise.
  • Reduced time-to-fill.
  • Steady pipeline of qualified candidates.
  • Flexibility and scalability to match business needs.
Engagement example
  • Fixed monthly retainer.
  • Recommended minimum engagement: 3 months.
  • Number of active roles agreed together with the client.
  • Regular reporting and KPIs agreed from the start of the engagement.
Example KPIs
  • Number of candidates presented.
  • Number of interviews scheduled.
  • Time-to-shortlist / Time-to-hire.
  • Number of hires made.
  • Offer acceptance rate.
